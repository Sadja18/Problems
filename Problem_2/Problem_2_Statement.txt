Problem Statement:
Given an integer N, calculate the sum of odd digits and sum of even digits of N.
Print sum_of_even_digits and sum_of_odd_digits as a space separrated string.

Sample Input: N = 12345
Sample Output: 6 9
Explanation: 
For N = 12345, 
Sum of Even Digits = 2 + 4 = 6
Sum of Odd Digits = 1 + 3 + 5 = 9
